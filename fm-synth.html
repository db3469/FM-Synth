FM-SYNTH
The FM Synth is an instrument doing frequency modulation synthesis. This is very different than our other synthesizers like the Simple, which is doing subtractive synthesis.
  • FM synthesis means that you create a complex waveform by modulating a simple waveform with another simple waveform.
  • This is done using waveforms of different frequencies and amplitudes to kind of mangle, distort and change the sound into something more complex and interesting.
We wont dig deep here in how to use it to create complex sounds. But you can find lots of great material on FM synthesis using a search engine!
